How to Play

Themed Codenames is a variant of the classic Codenames game, where the game words are customized by entering a theme of your choice! Each team (Red and Blue) takes turns to click cards (right click on a card to edit it). Revealing a card will show its assigned color:

  • Red or Blue cards: Increases the respective team's score (and the turn continues).
  • Neutral cards: Ends the turn without reward.
  • Bomb card: Instantly loses the game for the team that selected it.

Use the "Shuffle" button to restart the game with a new board, or "New Theme?" to return to the theme submission page.
